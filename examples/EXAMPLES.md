## 示例 1：病假（英文）

**场景**：学生因发烧无法参加明天的课程

**用户输入：**
```
我需要请假，明天无法上课，发烧，Professor Johnson，CS101，李明，学号 20231234
```

**生成的邮件：**

```
Subject: Absence from CS101 - Li Ming

Dear Professor Johnson,

I hope this email finds you well.

I am writing to inform you that I will be unable to attend your CS101 class tomorrow due to illness. I have been experiencing a high fever and need to rest at home.

I have already checked the syllabus to ensure I stay up to date with the course material. I will make sure to catch up on the lecture notes and submit any assignments by the deadline.

If there are any in-class activities or quizzes scheduled for tomorrow, please let me know if there is an alternative way to make up for them.

Thank you for your understanding. I apologize for any inconvenience this may cause.

Best regards,
Li Ming
20231234
CS101
```

## 示例 3：事假（英文）

**场景**：学生因家庭事务需要请假

**用户输入：**
```
我需要事假邮件，英文，家庭事务，下周一，Professor Williams，Economics 201，王小华，20239001
```

**生成的邮件：**

```
Subject: Request for Absence - Economics 201 - Wang Xiaohua

Dear Professor Williams,

I hope you are doing well.

I am writing to request permission to be absent from your Economics 201 class next Monday. I have a family commitment that requires my attendance during the scheduled class time.

I understand the importance of attending all classes, and I will ensure that I catch up on the material covered during my absence. I have arranged with a classmate to share their notes with me, and I will review the lecture slides on my own.

Please let me know if there are any specific requirements or assignments I should be aware of for that day.

Thank you for your consideration and understanding.

Sincerely,
Wang Xiaohua
20239001
Economics 201
```

```

- Complete all assigned readings before the next class
- Submit any due assignments in advance or electronically

Thank you for your support of my academic growth.

Best regards,
Zhao Lei
20231111
Machine Learning 501
```

## 💡 使用技巧

1. **提供完整信息**：教授姓名、课程名称、你的姓名和学号是必需的
2. **说明具体原因**：简要说明请假原因，但不必过于详细
3. **提前通知**：尽量提前发送邮件，给教授足够时间处理
4. **主动补救**：说明你会如何补上缺席的内容
5. **保持专业**：使用正式的语言和格式

## 📝 常见问题

**Q: 我应该提前多久发送请假邮件？**
A: 最好至少提前 24 小时。如果是紧急情况，尽快发送即可。

**Q: 我需要提供医疗证明吗？**
A: 取决于学校政策和教授要求。长期病假通常需要，短期病假可能不需要。

**Q: 邮件主题应该怎么写？**
A: 使用清晰的格式：`Absence from [课程名] - [你的姓名]` 或 `Request for Absence - [课程名] - [你的姓名]`

**Q: 我应该抄送给谁吗？**
A: 如果学校要求，可以抄送给系秘书或学术顾问。否则，直接发给教授即可。
